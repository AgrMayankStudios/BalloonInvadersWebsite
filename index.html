Description

Your mission is to pop the balloons that have invaded your living room. The game uses Augmented Reality to spawn balloons around you, after which you can look around to find them. Once they appear, pop the Balloons with a tap! There are multiple kinds of balloons, having different features and colours. Pop as many balloons as you can before the time gets over.
